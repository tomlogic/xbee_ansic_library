Detailed Description

Header for AT Commands sent to the local (serially-attached) XBee.

XBEE_FRAME_LOCAL_AT_CMD (0x08) or XBEE_FRAME_LOCAL_AT_CMD_Q (0x09) 1 to 255, or 0 to suppress the Transmit Status response two-character AT Command
